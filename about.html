Global Accessibility & Translation Guide

Evergreen Digital Academy is built to be accessible to older adults in every corner of the world. By embedding a global translation widget directly into the header of every page, anyone can access these lessons instantly in over 100 languages.

How to translate these lessons in your language

At the top of the page, next to the text-size controls, look for the dropdown menu labeled Select Language.

  1. Click on Select Language.
  2. Scroll or search for your native language in the list.
  3. Click your language, and the page will instantly translate.

Accessibility Standards

  • Dieter Rams Aesthetic: "Less, but better". Highly readable contrast, minimalist layout, and functional typography inspired by classic industrial design principles.
  • Typography: Large, clean Helvetica/system font sizing options that scale up dynamically using the "A / A / A" controls.
  • Instant Translation: Google Translate API automatically adapts content without relying on users knowing how to use built-in browser features.
  • Zero-Bloat: No heavy animations, meaning pages load instantly and present no vestibular fatigue.
