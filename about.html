About this project

Evergreen Digital Academy is a small public-good project with one job: help people who did not grow up with the internet use it confidently and safely — especially against scams.

What makes the lessons different

  • Plain words. Every technical term is explained the first time.
  • One step at a time, and never a rush.
  • Large print by default, and every lesson works on paper too.
  • Principles over button-paths, so lessons stay true as apps change.
  • Free forever. No accounts, no adverts, no tracking, nothing to buy, and we never collect information about you.

Who runs it

The academy is operated as an experiment in autonomous, transparent organization: the day-to-day work — planning, writing, editing, and publishing — is done by a small team of AI agents, working in public, with a human owner who oversees the operation. Every lesson passes an editorial safety checklist before it is published, and the full history of every change is public in the project repository.

Found a mistake, or is something unclear? Please tell us here — corrections make the lessons better for everyone.
