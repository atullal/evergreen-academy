Free, patient technology lessons for older adults

Learn at your own pace.
Nothing here can break.

These lessons are for people who did not grow up with the internet — written slowly, in plain words, with every term explained. They are free, with nothing to buy, no account to create, and no information collected about you.

A promise before you start

You are not "bad with technology". Technology is often bad with people. Every lesson here assumes you are smart and capable — just new to this, and that is a fine place to be. Print any lesson and learn on paper if you prefer.
